Pet Mania

Project Motivation

Pet Mania is essentially a photo sharing application which targets people who own pets, who is going to have pets, and basically people who like animals. Not only can users have a platform to share pictures of their own pets, but also they can view photos from other users. They can also search the photos, users and other information among the whole Pet Mania. For pet lovers, it can become a social method of vital importance. Ideally, Pet Mania is committed to the construction of an open pet information exchange community.

ER Diagram and Schema

User( username , password, DOB, gender, createDate, name, region, petBreed, petKind, petGender, petName)
Post(date, image, text, likes, POID , postBy)
following( follower , followee )

Functionality

We aim to build a social media application based on photo sharing of pets. Ideally, it will provide information exchanging between users and finally build a open community where users can also socialize with their pets.

User account register and login

Sign up account by completing basic information about both users and their pets. Login with the account you registered.

View post, post picture with text description and delete post

Users can post picture of their pets and add text description to the image.

Like and unlike post

Users can like and unlike posts, the number of likes will be recorded in every post.

Follow and unfollow other others

User can follow and unfollow other users. All the posts of users you followed will be showed at the homepage.

Search

In search page, users can get post they want by searching username or the text description attached to that post

Pet picture recommendation

In search page, we will recommend you the most popular posts among the community and also personalized posts according to what post you liked.
